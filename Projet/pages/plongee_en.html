Diving

Riou Archipelago (Marseille)

The Riou archipelago is a magnificent place for scuba diving, we offer diving at this place from July 1st to September 1st.
This place is perfect for beginners in scuba diving.

Golf of Porto (West of Corsica)

First dive sessions in shallower places and experienced dive sessions in deeper places (in the afternoon) at the Golf de Porto in western Corsica. We offer this service throughout the high season from June 20 to September 15.

Belle-île-en-mer (South of Bretagne)

Scuba diving baptism sessions in the south of Brittany at belle-île-en-mer,
perfect place for beginners as well as for the more experienced.
This service is offered all summer.

Boat week

Dive'in offers you to go diving with departure by boat with a variable duration according to your desires in one of its 3 magnificent frames offered above.
During this week we offer a lot of scuba diving including one to clean the seabed. Every evening, there will be themed evenings organized on the boat, with good local dishes.
