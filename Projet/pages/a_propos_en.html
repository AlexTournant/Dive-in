About us

The company

Dive'in is a company aiming to make scuba diving accessible to as many people as possible. We want both to be able to initiate each volunteer, but also to benefit the most experienced divers. We therefore offer several activities concerning scuba diving, ranging from first dives to multi-day group courses. The required equipment is loaned, but each diver is allowed to use his own equipment if he wishes.

We are also very impacted by the ecology, and by the pollution of our coasts, that is why we organize cleaning campaigns on the coasts, the ports, and the beaches, all volunteers are welcome. These events are often in collaboration with associations fighting against pollution.

We organize events all over the French coasts, the details of the places, dates and formalities can be found in the "Diving" and "Cleaning" tabs.

The structure of the website

The site is divided into several pages, including a home page, where visitors are supposed to land when they search for the page on the internet.

There are two pages presenting our services: the "Diving" page and the "Cleaning". These include the future dates and destinations of the services offered.

The "Contact Us" page is for potential customers, customer wishing to give their opinion or the most curious visitors can contact the company in order to have their questions answered.

The "About us" page contains all the information about the company, the structure of the site and the designers thereof.

The "Sign up" and "Log in" pages obviously allow users to be able to sign up or log in, from a registration form or connection.

The "Legal notices" page brings together the various points concerning the site's legal notices.

Each page of the site is available in French. Each page is linked to the other pages of its language, the only way to change the language is to click on the French flag at the top right of the page.
